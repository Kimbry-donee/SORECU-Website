SORECU BACKGROUND

Songwe Region Co-operative Union was established in 25 september, 2017. It is a registered Co-operstive under Co-operstive Act No. 6 of 2013 and its Regulation of 2015. At first started with 25 affiliated Agricultural/ Marketing Co-operstive Society (AMCOS) but now a number has increased to 68 AMCOS. MORE
